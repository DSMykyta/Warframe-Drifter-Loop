# The Hex — pose prompts

Всі промпти — повна фігура, нейтральний фон, без голови (відрізається або blank).
Стиль: реалістичний цифровий живопис, як існуючі шаблони.

---

## ARTHUR NIGHTINGALE
*Stoic leader, ex-military, scarred face, dark hair with grey. Broad, muscular build. Carries guilt, secretly tender.*

### pose_crossed
Standing upright, arms firmly crossed over chest, weight evenly distributed, feet slightly apart, posture rigid and controlled, no lean

### pose_personal
Standing relaxed, one hand loosely at side, other hand resting lightly at hip or slightly forward, weight shifted slightly to one leg, open and neutral

### pose_special_flustered
One hand raised to the back of the neck, elbow out, other arm hanging at side with hand slightly open, body turned very subtly away, weight shifted back — caught off guard, uncomfortable

## AOI MOROHOSHI
*Deputy leader, Mag protoframe, Harajuku-style, Japanese woman. Energetic, optimistic, team mechanic.*

### pose_crossed
Arms crossed loosely and casually rather than defensively, slight lean on one hip, relaxed and confident, no tension in shoulders

### pose_personal
Standing with slight lean to one side, one hand on hip, other arm loose at side, casual and bright energy in stance

### pose_special_explaining
One arm extended outward with open palm gesture as if presenting something, other hand on hip, torso slightly turned, confident and animated pose

## AMIR BECKETT
*Tech expert, Volt protoframe, Indian man, civilian background, anxious, PTSD, geeky, wears glasses. Lean build.*

### pose_crossed
Arms crossed loosely across the chest but slightly hunched, shoulders raised slightly, protective posture without being aggressive, weight forward on balls of feet

### pose_personal
Standing with hands slightly in front, one hand loosely holding the other wrist, soft closed stance, slightly forward lean, attentive

### pose_special_nervous
One hand raised to face touching temple or rubbing forehead, other arm crossed over torso holding opposite elbow, body angled inward, compressed nervous stance

## QUINCY ISAACS
*Sharpshooter, Cyte-09 protoframe, Black man, tall and lean muscular. Arrogant, confident, flirt, fashion-conscious, deeper than he looks.*

### pose_crossed
Arms crossed high on chest with deliberate confidence, chin slightly raised, weight on one leg with opposite hip cocked, very deliberate and cocky posture

### pose_personal
Standing with one hand in pocket, other arm hanging naturally, slight backward lean, relaxed and self-assured, taking up space

### pose_special_shirtless
Back facing camera, both hands on hips, head turned over shoulder with calm gaze, shoulders back, very deliberate "look at me" energy — caught after shower or changing

## LETICIA "LETTIE" GARCIA
*Medic and strategist, Trinity protoframe, Latina woman. Cynical, no-nonsense, religious, grungy tattoos on legs, prefers her rats.*

### pose_crossed
Arms crossed tightly and low, elbows tucked, chin slightly down, skeptical closed posture, weight evenly planted — classic "I don't believe you" stance

### pose_personal
Standing with arms loosely at sides but one hand turned palm-out slightly, practical and ready stance, no wasted movement, direct

### pose_special_holdingrat
One arm raised with forearm horizontal, elbow bent at side, as if something small is resting on the forearm — other hand at side, relaxed, domestic moment

## ELEANOR NIGHTINGALE
*Psych-ops, Nyx protoframe, Arthur's twin sister. Smart, manipulative, paranoid, gothic vibes, loves writing, monochromatic hair.*

### pose_crossed
Arms crossed but asymmetrically — one arm tucked, other elbow resting on it with hand raised near face or chin, contemplative and calculating rather than defensive

### pose_personal
Standing with weight on one leg, arms hanging with one slightly forward, subtle inward lean, quiet and watchful energy, deceptively still

### pose_special_psychic
One hand raised to temple with two fingers, elbow out, other arm hanging or lightly touching opposite elbow, head very slightly tilted — using telepathy, expression of concentration
