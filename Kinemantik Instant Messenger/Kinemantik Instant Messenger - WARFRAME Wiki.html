Kinemantik Instant Messenger

The Kinemantik Instant Messager (KIM) is a communication and chat service created by Kinemantik in 1999, allowing users to communicate with individuals or in chat rooms. It is available after completing The Hex quest.

Overview

[edit | edit source]

KIM is often used by the citizens of Höllvania to communicate and distribute information. The Drifter gains access to KIM to socialize. It is available after completion of The Hex Quest and can be accessed using the Pom-2 computer in the Base of Operations.

The characters are separated into the following groups:

  •  The Hex: Arthur, Aoi, Amir, Quincy, Leticia, and Eleanor.
  • The Roundtable: Flare, Minerva, Velimir, and Kaya.
    • Flare becomes available after reaching Rank 4 - Hot & Fresh with The Hex Syndicate.
    • Minerva, Velimir, and Kaya becomes available after reaching Rank 5 - Pizza Party.
  • The Devil's Triad: Lyon, Marie, and Roathe.
    • Marie and Roathe require completion of The Old Peace quest.
    • Lyon requires Marie at "Liked" status, and will need to be spoken to in person to trigger his first conversation.

Romance & Relationship

[edit | edit source]

KIM's primary feature is the Romance & Relationship System that allows the Drifter to form budding relationships with the Hex and the Devil's Triad, learning more about their backstories, the history of Höllvania, the Orokin, and their relationships with the other members of their respective groups.

Optionally, the Drifter may pursue an individual member of the Hex and the Devil's Triad as a love interest. With a romanced member of the Hex, they spend New Years Eve 1999 with their significant other, culminating in a kiss with them. With a romanced member of the Devil's Triad, the Drifter can choose to "spend some alone time" with with their significant other, culminating in a kiss with them and the romanced Devil's Triad member can send Captura Date Scenes to meet the Drifter in.

KIM Profiles

[edit | edit source]

Each Hex member has a profile that displays their username, real name, birthday, and current Chemistry rank. The Notes section changes as Chemistry rank increases with the selected Hex member, giving insights to help in conversation and gift choices. The clock icon on the profile screen allows the player to view logs of previous conversations. Note that this does not allow choices to be changed later; if the player wishes to change what was said, they must choose to reset the chat system at the end of the month.

Chemistry

[edit | edit source]

Interacting with KIM increases Chemistry with individual members.

Players can increase their Chemistry by chatting, gifting, and running Bounties with the original members of the Hex, while for the members of the Roundtable and the Devil's Triad, Chemistry can only be increased by chatting. All activities are available once per day resetting at the Daily Reset. To start earning Chemistry requires completing an introductory chat with each Hex member. Chemistry cannot be lost due to dialogue choices or non-favoured gifts. However, certain dialogue choices can lock the player out of further conversations with a given character.

When striking up conversation, choices that grant Chemistry are indicated by the text appearing with a golden (for the Hex and Roundtable) or red (for the Devil's Triad) gradient effect.

Chemistry ranks are as follows:

  1. Neutral
  2. Friendly (requires 40 Chemistry, 2 Dialogues, and Rank 2 - Fresh Slice with  The Hex)
  3. Liked (requires 90 Chemistry, 4 Dialogues, and Rank 3 - 2-for-1)
    • Reaching Liked with all six members is required to trigger The Hex Finale and unlock Rank 4 - Hot & Fresh with  The Hex.
  4. Trusted (requires 160 Chemistry, 7 Dialogues, and Rank 4 - Hot & Fresh)
  5. Close (requires 250 Chemistry, 10 Dialogues, and Rank 5 - Pizza Party)
  6. Best Friends (requires 360 Chemistry and 13 Dialogues)
    • Loved - If the player chooses to pursue a relationship with one of the Hex and/or the Devil's Triad
    • Associate - For Flare, Minerva, Velimir, or Kaya if the player fails to resolve their personal issues

If the player does not have the required Syndicate standing to rank up with a member of the Hex, a lock symbol will appear on the Hex member's chat tab, along with a note stating I need to prove myself as a member of the Hex before I spend more time talking to them one on one..." However, players can continue to earn Chemistry points even if they are capped by the Syndicate standing; any excess points will flow into the next Chemistry rank once the Syndicate is ranked up.

If the player does not have the required Chemistry to rank up, but has no more conversations available at their current rank, the results will differ based on which protoframe it is.

  • Hex: A lock symbol and the message Requires More Chemistry will appear on their chat tab, and a note will be added to their profile stating "I need to increase my chemistry with them before I can keep talking one on one."

Bounties

[edit | edit source]

Each Bounty in the Höllvania Central Mall has a randomly assigned Hex member that will join the player in-mission, and is guaranteed to award Chemistry upon completion. Every bounty grants 20 Chemistry regardless of Bounty Tier. Members are reassigned every 150-minute bounty reset.

Note that the accompany member will not participate in the mission when providing transmissions:

  • Arthur will not accompany Assassination.
  • Aoi for Scaldra Exterminate.
  • Amir for Scaldra Hell-Scrub.
  • Quincy for Techrot Exterminate.
  • Leticia for Techrot Hell-Scrub.
  • Eleanor for Legacyte Harvest.

The Roundtable and the Devil's Triad will not appear in Bounties and as such Chemistry cannot be earned this way for them.

Dating

[edit | edit source]

After reaching "Trusted" with Aoi, Amir or Quincy, or "Close" with Arthur, Leticia, Eleanor, Marie, Roathe, or Lyon, the option to "Ask to date" will appear when speaking to the corresponding members in the Höllvania Central Mall (The Hex) and La Cathédrale (The Devil's Triad). Depending on chatting responses in KIM, the recipient may or may not accept the invitation to date. It is also possible to start or end relationships during KIM chats. The Roundtable cannot be dated, however.

Only one Hex and one Devil's Triad member can be dated at a time - should the player wish to pursue other relationships within the same group, they will need to break up with their current partner first. Breaking up with a member of either group directly will allow the Drifter to date them again at a later point if they wish so. However, if the break up was reached through the KIM chats, said Hex and/or Devil's Triad member cannot be dated again without resetting their conversations on KIM chat or through certain specific KIM chats.

After reaching "Loved" with the player's date, the Hex member will appear in the Backroom, while the Devil's Triad member presents an option to "share some time alone", which is a cutscene showing the Drifter and their Devil's Triad date sharing a kiss with a 7-day cooldown. The romanced Devil's Triad member can also send Captura Date Scenes to meet the player in.

When the 1999 Calendar year rolls over to the Winter season every 4 real-life weeks and the player has reached "Loved" with one of the Hex members, the player will receive an inbox message containing a cutscene showing the Drifter and their Hex date sharing a kiss on New Year's Eve. If the player does not have a Hex date but has "Best Friends" with all of the initial six members of the Hex, the Drifter will instead share a hug with Kalymos.

Digital Grimoire (Starting Over)

[edit | edit source]

“Select a Batch to reset your conversations with its respective Protoframes.”

—In-Game Description

The Digital Grimoire, available in the bottom right corner of the POM-2 interface, offers the player to reset their conversation history with the Hex (Arthur, Aoi, Eleanor, Amir, Quincy, and Leticia), the Roundtable (Flare, Kaya, Velimir & Minerva), or the Devil's Triad (Marie, Roathe, Lyon). Choosing to reset will erase all conversation history and Chemistry ranks. The player's Standing with  The Hex, the Hex group chat, and Roathe's The Descendia voice messages are unaffected by the reset. Resetting has a cooldown of 28 days.

Starting over notably affects certain conversations with Lizzie (Flare) and Roathe, who become aware of the Drifter's ability to loop time and reset their memories.

This option becomes unlocked on the POM-2 after completing The Hex Finale, unlocked after reaching Rank 3 - 2-for-1 with The Hex and reaching Liked status with all six original members of The Hex.

Captura Scene Dates:

Sometimes, for romance to bloom, you need to get away from the world.

Once you have reached Loved with your Protoframe, they will send you a Captura Date Scene to your inbox every week. Each Protoframe has five Dates that you can earn in this way.

These Date Scenes allow you to spend some one-on-one time with your beau, and learn more about them through their Scene-exclusive voice lines. You can make use of the Captura tools to take photos with your beloved, or simply enjoy the pleasure of their company.

If you choose to break up with your Protoframe, you will retain any Captura Date Scenes you have earned — and you can continue to use them to revisit your old flames, as you desire. We won’t tell! Additionally, you will keep any Scenes you own upon resetting The Devil’s Triad’s memories*.

* Certain voice lines in these Date Scenes are tied to your conversation choices with the Protoframe, so you may see a change in dialogue after resetting.

Consent Across Spacetime:

The Hex were the first dateable characters added to the game, so introducing a new group of romanceable characters may give devoted Drifters some pause. We could get into the weeds with technicalities here — eternalism, etc. — but at the end of the day you can now successfully woo and smooch two individuals at the same time.

For those interested in taking this path, dating both a Hex and Devil’s Triad Protoframe will trigger a KIM conversation with each individual where they give consent for the Drifter to have multiple relationships.

Update 38.5 (2025-03-19)

KIM UPDATES

New KIM Conversations: Kaya, Minerva & Velimir, Flare

There are some new faces around town! The latest Protoframe arrivals not only have The Hex chatting but also… you! Visit the Pom-2 PC in your Base of Operations and hop into the KIM messaging system to start chatting with Flare, Minerva & Velimir (together in a group chat), and Kaya.

Discover what is haunting Flare and their sorrowful past. Learn more about the tumultuous history between Minerva and Velimir in their group chat as well as their relation to Major Neci Rusalka. Uncover why Kaya is desperate to escape and start fresh far far away from 1999.

Prerequisites:

  • Access to the Pom-2 PC by completing The Hex Quest
  • Conversations with Flare are available at Rank 4 (Hot & Fresh) with The Hex Syndicate
  • Conversations with Kaya and the group chat with Minerva and Velimir are available at Rank 5 (Pizza Party) with the Hex Syndicate

New The Hex group chat conversations will also be available after the first time you speak to each of the new Protoframes.

Chatting & Rapport

Like The Hex, you can chat with each of the new Protoframes once per day (resetting at 0:00 UTC).

However, unlike The Hex, they are not romanceable. The conversations will have you learning about these characters through the difficult and complex situations they have found themselves in. The narrative goal for you as the Drifter is to help them navigate these trials and mend what's been broken (if you so chose to go that route!). But it won’t be easy and will require you to choose your words carefully in order for them to gain trust with you. With that said, discussions may veer into sensitive topics like mental health, loss, grief, and more.

Since the goal is not to romance, the Chemistry you earn is more about building rapport with these new Protoframes. If your answers bode well, the messages will have the same gold shimmery text to indicate that you are on the right path. Gifting items from the DirectGiftz Shop to these Protoframes will not increase your Chemistry Rank, as that is tied specifically to the romance system. But who doesn’t love a good Scooticle, right? For that same reason, these Protoframes will also not appear in Bounties.

As you progress through the conversations and gain rapport, their status with you will change. Unlike The Hex, these conversations occur in a set order, and your Rank will change as you progress through their story.

  • Neutral
  • Friendly
  • Liked
  • Trusted
  • Close
  • Associate (if you didn’t succeed at helping them overcome their issue)
  • Best Friends (if you succeed at helping them overcome their issue)

You can view your rank with them in their KIM Profiles. Their profiles will also update with notes as you continue to chat with them to help guide you on what the “correct” replies may be.

There will be a point in these conversations, where it’ll be clear you have either succeeded or failed in helping to resolve their issues. If you have made all the right choices to help them with their problems there will be certain “events” that occur to indicate that you have been successful in your endeavors. But there’s no fun in spoiling those surprises, so you’ll just have to play through to experience the fruits of your comradery and consulting!

Birthday Messages

Additional conversations are available by connecting with the new Protoframes on their birthdays! Their dates will appear in the 1999 Calendar at Rank 5 (Pizza Party) with The Hex Syndicate. While there is no need to gain Chemistry with these Protoframes, these messages serve as an additional way to connect with — or antagonize — these new characters.

NOTE: With the way 1999 Calendar Seasons are generated, new Protoframe birthdays will not appear on the Calendar until the New Year on April 7 at 0:00 UTC.

Starting Over

When the 1999 Calendar resets every month, choosing to forget or remember will also apply to the conversations you have with Kaya, Minerva & Velimir and Flare. For those interested in experiencing all of the possibilities in these new chats, choosing to Forget will reset all conversations (Hex included) back to square one. On that note, we’ve also added a new setting that allows all KIM conversations to play, learn more in General KIM Improvements (read on for details).

General KIM Improvements:

  • The Pom-2 now has the following settings that you can access by selecting the gear icon next to the power button in the bottom right:
  • Play All KIM Conversations Toggle: All available conversations will play in random order within each rank before moving on to the next rank. Reaching higher chemistry will take more time with this toggled on.
    • Once the toggle is enabled, all conversation will play within your current Rank. So for example, if you are in the Close with Arthur and enable this toggle, all of the conversations in Close (and beyond while enabled) will play. If you wish to play ALL the conversations across all ranks, you will need to forget memories at the 1999 year reset.
  • Jump to Next Player Interaction Point Toggle: Disables the typing animation of Protoframes within the KIM system, automatically jumping ahead to the next player interaction point in all dialogues.

Background Selection: Choose from a variety of exciting background images!

  • The Reset or Remember pop-up at 1999 year-end will now only appear when visiting the KIM section of the Pom-2.
    • In Hotfix 38.0.8, we added the ability to back out of the reset popup if you weren’t ready to make a decision, but it continued to appear each time you opened up the Pom-2 which could be intrusive to players who were not looking to interact with the KIM system every time. So we changed it to only appear when you select the KIM icon!
