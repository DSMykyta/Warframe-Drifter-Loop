End of The Old Peace

[edit | edit source]
  • POM2_LAB1: Drifter. Forgive me for the impropriety of this imposition, I did not wish to contact you using this device. But this is verging on an emergency and I did not know to whom else I could turn.

    • > Who is this...?
  • POM2_LAB1: Ah, yes. This is still on default settings. This is Loid. I should have... led with that, but matters here in the Sanctum Anatomica have been deeply chaotic of late.
  • POM2_LAB1: Which is why I am contacting you. I am in need of your assistance. Three protoframes have arrived here, in the Sanctum, in my time. And not ones previously known to us.
  • POM2_LAB1: One of them is an
  • POM2_LAB1: Apologies
  • POM2_LAB1: One of them is Orokin, Drifter.

      • POM2_LAB1: The Lotus and the Operator have other matters to attend to, believe me. They have been taxed well and truly enough to their limits as it is. I would not ask you to help me with these matters if I felt I had anywhere else to turn.
    • > Oh no...
      • POM2_LAB1: I would not ask you to help me with these matters if I felt I had anywhere else to turn.
    • > What?!
      • POM2_LAB1: I would not ask you to help me with these matters if I felt I had anywhere else to turn.
  • POM2_LAB1: You are... seemingly the new expert in matters of this kind.
  • POM2_LAB1: I do not know how to best describe them. But if you come to the Sanctum, I believe it will be hard to miss them. Two of them have agreed to speak to you here on the POM-2. One may need some further convincing.
